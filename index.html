APPETIZER

Have you ever wanted to go out, but didn’t know what restaurant to choose? Found Google Map’s recommendations unhelpful or unwieldy? Been suspicious of Yelp reviews’ reliability?

Reviews can contain lots of bias - we all have different preferences in food, service, ambiance, and more.

We aim to look at the different kinds of sentiments present in reviews as well as general trends across restaurants, cuisines, and locations.

Hopefully, you’ll be able to find a restaurant you’d like to visit by the end of this site!
